Goalie - Meet Your Goals

Goalie is a web app that quantifies your goals and lets your measure your progress

  • Make a list measureable goals
  • Record a score for each goal every day
  • Use the long term data to keep yourself on track!

The concept for this website is based off Marshall Goldsmith's book, Triggers: Creating Behavior That Lasts - Becoming the Person You Want to Be. You can pick it up on Amazon here.
